La casa del cibo: A heaven in earth for italian food lovers.

Since our modest beginnings in 2004 with a little space in Lac 2 Tunis, La casa del cibo development has been enlivened with the energy to cook and serve Italian plates.

  • Our Restaurant is the best italian Restaurant in tunisia.
  • Our goal is to give you a unique experience that will not break the bank.
  • Our chefs are put through an in-depth interview process and testing. They are servsafe certified, insured and experienced.

Why Us

Why Choose Our Restaurant

01

Friendly environment

Our motto is to make the atmosphere friendly and as efficient as possible. The waiters are patiently and happily take your order and serve the meal in the estimated time. A nice and lovely environment forces you to come back for another date.

02

Perfect food presentation

In a food restaurant, food plating is a central idea to keep guests happy as they experience your restaurant. People scan the taste before eating, so our creative plating can enhance both the appearance and the flavor of the food.

03

Takeaway facility

We’re not limited our options to just have a meal at our place. You’re most welcomed if you want to throw a party at your home and want our best Italian food in Tunisia to please your guests.
